Omnipotence Paradox

“Can an Omnipotent God create a stone that he cannot lift?” For both Yes and No, God no longer remains Omnipotent (i.e. One who can do everything) • Proof by the above paradox is fallacious. Why : You need to understand Set theory and Symbolic logic to comprehend the discrepancy.
